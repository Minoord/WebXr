About the WebXR

WebXR is a function on this website where you can see

the working place of Folkert de Jong in 3D. Use the

WASD Buttons on your keyboard to move around and hold

your left mouse button and move your mouse to look around

About LAM

If you want to know more about LAM or if you want to
